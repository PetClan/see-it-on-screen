Ice Melts

Short Film • 2022

Synopsis

A contemplative exploration of change, loss, and the passage of time. Ice Melts captures fleeting moments of transformation with poetic imagery and emotional resonance.

Through careful observation and intimate storytelling, the film invites viewers to reflect on the impermanence of all things and the beauty found in transition.

Format: Short Film
Year: 2022
Genre: Drama
