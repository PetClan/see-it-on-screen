26.2

Drama • 2024

Synopsis

A powerful drama exploring the determination and resilience of the human spirit. Through the lens of a marathon runner's journey, 26.2 examines themes of perseverance, personal struggle, and the pursuit of seemingly impossible goals.

Set against the backdrop of training and competition, the film delves into the psychological and physical challenges that test the limits of what one person can endure.
