About Us

Talent exists everywhere. What's missing are the opportunities to discover and nurture it.

We create pathways into film production across Scotland. We do this by actually making films.

See It On Screen and See It Off Productions are dedicated to democratising access to the film industry. We believe that filmmaking shouldn't be reserved for those with connections or formal training‚Äîit should be open to anyone with passion, dedication, and a willingness to learn.

Our Approach

Every film project we undertake blends professionals and amateurs, creating a unique learning environment where experience meets enthusiasm. This isn't about watching from the sidelines‚Äîit's about getting your hands dirty, learning on set, and contributing to real productions.

We don't ask for portfolios, showreels, or years of experience. All we ask is that you get in touch, tell us you want to be involved, and turn up on time. Your reliability and commitment are what matter most.

What We Support

We support both narrative and documentary filmmaking. Whether you want to work in front of the camera or behind it, whether you're interested in directing, cinematography, sound, editing, or any other aspect of production‚Äîthere's a place for you here.

Have an idea for a screenplay? We want to hear it. Have a story that needs to be told? Let's discuss how to bring it to the screen. We're committed to nurturing new voices and fresh perspectives in Scottish cinema.
